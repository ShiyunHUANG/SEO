Who We Are

Carnegie Financial Services (CFS) is an online electronic banking service provider. We offer a lot of financial related premier services for individuals and organizations. For individual users, we provide eBanking services, such as no fee bank account service (free checking and high rate saving), all kinds of personal low interest loans including education loan, housing loan and transportation loan, high yield saving services which provide highest interest rate compare to all our other competitors. For companies and big organization users, our services are provided as free checking account, high rate saving account, high yield certificates of deposit (CDs) and most importantly, mortgage services including large business mortgages, jumbo company mortgages. Our satisfied customers range from major corporations to wealthy individuals and to large numbers of professionals. Companies like Moffet Foods and Big Red Tractors are all our customers and we also provide customized banking services perfectly designed for educators, junior faculties, students and all income classes.

Our Founder : The late Dr. Harry Q. Bovik had the vision to make all banking transactions electronic way back in the 1980's. After many difficult years, Dr. Bovik made eBanking really work. Now CFS is proud to be the premier integrated online banking company providing electronic banking account services, loan services, mortgage services, certificate of deposit services and more financial services.
